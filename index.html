About Us

At Age Concern Southend, we are committed to supporting and improving the lives of elderly individuals in our community. We provide a range of vital services to help older people maintain their independence, dignity, and overall well-being.

Our Services Include:

  • Home support – Helping with household tasks, personal care, and mobility assistance.
  • Social activities – Organizing events, clubs, and outings to keep older people socially connected.
  • Information and advice – Providing crucial legal, financial, and health-related advice to navigate aging with confidence.
  • Advocacy – Representing older people’s rights and ensuring their voices are heard in the community.

Join us in making a difference in the lives of older individuals in Southend.
