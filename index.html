About Age Concern

Age Concern Southend is a small, independent charity based in Westcliff-on-Sea, Essex. They provide support, advice, and services to older people in the Southend area.

Their mission is to help improve quality of life and foster community through various initiatives and services, including befriending, transport assistance, and a specialist dementia day service at their Haven Community Hub.

The Services

They provide:

  • Telephone and face-to-face befriending services
  • Transport support for appointments and shopping
  • Community clubs and social events
  • Dementia Day Centre sessions for enhanced health and wellbeing
